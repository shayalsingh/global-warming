How can we help prevent Global Warming?

We could help by not using cars as much as we do and walking or biking instead.

We could save so much carbon dioxide by just recycling half of our household waste.

Time to make a change

After all these years we have finally decided to take global warming seriously as it is more visible than it has ever been before.

It is easy to talk about change but it is hard to do, we must change our lifestyle so that the future generations can follow in our footsteps.
